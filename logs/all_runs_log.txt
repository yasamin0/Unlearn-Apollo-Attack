Important methodological note:
- The trustworthy Apollo metric is NOT the raw final-step un_conf / ov_conf based AUC.
- The correct Apollo evaluation must be computed from ternary threshold sweep outputs:
  - full_classifications
  - threshold_data
  - tpr_results
- Therefore, the valid AUC is the empirical one-vs-rest AUC computed from the ternary sweep.
- This correction was verified by inspecting the attack logic and ternary result files.

==================================================
IMPORTANT SEED CLARIFICATION
==================================================

During attack execution, logs show:
Unlearn Arguments Loaded: ... seed=42

This does NOT mean the new attack run ignored the new seed.

Meaning:
- the displayed seed=42 belongs to the saved target/unlearn pipeline
- that seed comes from the original pretrain/unlearn artifacts stored in target_path
- the attack seed is the one passed directly in the attack command:
  - run1 -> seed 42
  - run2 -> seed 1
  - run3 -> seed 2
  - run4 -> seed 3

Therefore:
- runs 2/3/4 were genuinely executed with different ATTACK seeds
- but they still used the same underlying pretrained/unlearned target model and same saved shadow models
